London

It is the capital and largest city of the United Kingdom. Located on the River Thames in southern Britain, the city has a large number of universities, institutes, museums and theaters. Many international organizations and global companies are also headquartered in the city. Many Londoners enjoy going to the upscale markets on the streets

Tourist attractions

  • Big Ben
  • Buckingham Palace
  • London Eye

Additional Information

London is known for its rich history, black taxis, and double-decker buses.
